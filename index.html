I'm Mir Moeez — a full-stack developer, designer, and educator based in Srinagar, Kashmir. What started as curiosity about computers evolved into a multi-year journey building real products for real people.

Since 2021, I've freelanced across the full spectrum of web work — designing systems, writing backends, and shipping polished frontends for clients spanning personal brands to business platforms. I care deeply about the intersection of aesthetics and function.

Beyond building, I've spent years in the classroom. At Swift Pvt ITI and now Skyline Computers, I teach programming, web technologies, database management, and operating systems — helping the next generation of developers find their footing in tech.

Currently pursuing BCA Honours at Caset College of Computer Science, I continue to grow, collaborate, and push what's possible through digital craft.
